Welcome to Galaga!

You may think you know this classic game, but now there's a new version!

Controls
---------

Press A to begin the game (Z on a keyboard).

Use the left, right, up, and down arrow keys to move your rocket around the screen.

Press B to shoot (X on a keyboard).

You can go back to the start screen at any time by pressing Select (Backspace on a keyboard).


Directions
----------

Shoot at the aliens.

You get 20 points for every alien you shoot.

When all the aliens are dead, you have won the round.
